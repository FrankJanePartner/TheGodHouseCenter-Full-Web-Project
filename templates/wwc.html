Whole Word Conference 2024

This year's conference will be a school of ministry, with emphasis on the GIFTS AND CALLING of God, its bearing on the gospel commission, and its implications on the local church ministry.

We shall examine the apostolic precepts and examples - its discovery, recovery, development, exercise, application, transference, accountability, multiplicity, mentorship and mastery of the gifts and calling of God.

Get ready for a time of teaching and supernatural ministries, with indelible activations and Impartations, and the right hand of fellowship.

The Spirit says COME!
