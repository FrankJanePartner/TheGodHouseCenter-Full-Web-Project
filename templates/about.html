Our Vision
We exist to spread the true Gospel of our Lord and Savior Jesus Christ to the whole world, with the aim of transforming sinners into saints and guiding believers in the understanding of Christ. Our ultimate objective is to raise and equip end-time ministers who are fully committed to sharing the Gospel, freeing people from the kingdom of darkness, and leading them into the kingdom of God.
We Pray
At Godhouse, we never underestimate the power of prayer in sharing the gospel. We understand that the eyes of unbelievers have been blinded by the devil. Therefore, we continually pray for the souls of men to be delivered from the kingdom of darkness into the kingdom of Light, and for the Lord of the harvest to send out laborers into His harvest.
We Go
Our belief is that in order for individuals to be saved, they must listen to the Gospel. However, without a preacher, how can they hear it? Therefore, we strive to spread the word of Christ to all corners of the world, and guide people towards becoming his disciples.
We Give
We are totally sold out to Christ! We recognize that the money and resources entrusted to us by God are simply tools that enable us to spread His message to those who are yet to hear it. With this understanding, we give generously and joyfully to every ministry endeavor.
