About Shekinah Voices
Shekinah Voices is a group of worship ministers, instrumentalists, and pastors dedicated to spreading the Gospel of Christ through music. As an extension of Godhouse's mission to build a community of believers passionate about serving God, we are focused on creating spirit-filled music with lyrics rooted in the Scriptures. Our goal is to inspire listeners to deepen their relationship with Jesus.
