Running with the Vision Conference (RVC) is an annual believers’ conference organized by The Godhouse Centre. This year’s conference is themed: “Labourers With God". In this conference you will learn the undiluted word of God as it concerns our labour in and for God’s kingdom while in the body.
“Your viability for ministry is the spirit within.”
- Apostle T.D Philips, Convener

RUNNING WITH THE VISION CONFERENCE 2024

RVC is a school of ministry of the Gospel in teachings and supernatural manifestation in the spirit and by the spirit. This year’s conference will cover the following:

  • The believers’ role in the ministering of the Gospel.
  • The gifts and calling of God in the advancement of the Gospel.
  • We shall examine and practice the wisdom of flowing with the Spirit in ministering healings and working of miracles, with 100% results.
  • We shall highlight the wisdom supernatural interventions in provision and preservation, and much more.
